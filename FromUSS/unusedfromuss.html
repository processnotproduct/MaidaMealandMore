... and Beyond

  • Looping Ferry System
  • Environmental Monitoring
  • Rescue Vehicle
Today's space transportation system uses a point to point or "Out and Back" approach when traveling to a destination like the moon. This practice requires a lot of energy for acceleration and breaking that once used is forever lost.

The solar exploring habitat could be launched into a continuous elliptical orbit between any two objects. This will result in the conservation of the launch energy required, with only minor corrections required occasionally. The continuously looping space ferry system would be met by space taxis that would load and unload cargo and passengers at each destination.
Earth's climate is dramatically changing today due to manmade and natural processes. These changes in our planets climate will have a profound effect on billions of people who live next to water as sea levels rise due to the melting of the polar caps and other bodies of ice worldwide.

Monitoring and potentially manipulating our climate is of paramount importance and requires immediate action to avert trillions of dollars of losses and human suffering.
A smaller version of the solar exploring habitat can be produced and used as a rescue vehicle. One or more of them could remain in orbit near occupied areas to provide emergency response to ship repair, emergency evacuation, emergency rescue, medical response.

Platforms

  • Advanced Research
  • Bio-Medical Research
  • Mining Vehicle
  • Manufacturing
The planet orbiting habitat can be configured into any kind of advanced research platform where the research may be too dangerous to perform on earth or require special environmental conditions:
  • Astronomy
  • Energy Research
  • Hi-energy physics
  • Biological and genetic research
The advantage of space based research is safety, terrestrial assets, both human and material, are kept safe.
The main advantage of the planet orbiting habitat is its ability to create a variable gravity. Bio-medical research and manufacturing could use this benefit of the variable gravity in their processes; this will result in products that are not currently available.
Our solar system is extremely rich in natural resources from minerals and metals to gases. While the moon is an obvious first target, the asteroid belt and the gas giants represent the mother lode.

The solar exploring habitat can be configured for long term exploration, mining, and processing. The finished product can be transported to a looping space ferry for transport back to earth for manufacturing.
USS intends to build manufacturing platforms in Low Earth Orbit (LEO) to product unique products that can only be made in space due to the microgravity environment. These products will be initially pharmaceuticals and nanomaterials.

Eventually once in-situ materials from space are available USS will build processing and manufacturing platforms in space for a wide variety of products for in space use and for use on Earth.
